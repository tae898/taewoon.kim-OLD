What do I want?

Currently, my research includes cognitive science, natural language processing, knowledge representation and reasoning, and reinforcement learning. More specifically, I am working on creating a machine, agent, AI, robot, or whatever you call it, which has a human-like memory system. I believe creating such a machine allows us to understand and work with them better than otherwise.

I like scientific and engineering challenges in general. Not only that, I especially like when I can get inspirations from the fields that I’m not so familiar with (e.g., cognitive science, linguistics, etc.). I’m always up for challenges.
